Project Execution

The KLM Hospital Ventilation Upgrade Project focused on improving air quality, energy efficiency, and patient safety through the installation of advanced ventilation and air-handling systems.

Our team worked within an active hospital environment, ensuring minimal disruption to daily operations while meeting strict health and safety regulations. The project involved dismantling outdated units, installing new high-efficiency ventilation systems, and integrating smart controls for precise airflow management across various hospital departments.

Key Achievements

Innovative Design

Replaced aging ventilation infrastructure with state-of-the-art HVAC equipment to enhance indoor air quality and system reliability.

Safety Excellence

Executed all work under stringent hospital safety protocols, meeting both national and international health facility standards.

On-Time Delivery

Completed major upgrade phases without interrupting hospital operations, maintaining strict timelines through careful planning and coordination.
